Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 26, 2026

Summary: Clean App respects your privacy. We do not collect, store, or share any of your personal data. All processing happens locally on your device.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Clean App ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience using our App.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Clean App is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Photos, videos, or media files from your device
  • Contact information or address book data
  • Calendar events or scheduling information
  • Location data or GPS information
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Any data that leaves your device

3. How the App Works

Clean App helps you organize and clean up your device by:

  • Similar Photos: Analyzing photos locally on your device using Apple's Vision framework to find similar images. No photos are uploaded to any server.
  • Duplicate Contacts: Scanning your contacts locally to identify duplicates. Contact data never leaves your device.
  • Calendar Cleanup: Identifying spam or subscription calendar events locally. Calendar data is processed entirely on your device.

All analysis and processing happens entirely on your device. No data is transmitted to external servers.

4. Device Permissions

The App may request the following permissions:

  • Photo Library Access: To scan for similar photos. We only read photo metadata and visual features; we do not upload or share your photos.
  • Contacts Access: To identify duplicate contacts. Contact data is processed locally and never transmitted.
  • Calendar Access: To find spam calendar events. Calendar data remains on your device.

You can revoke these permissions at any time through your device's Settings app.

5. Subscriptions and Payments

If you purchase a subscription through the App:

  • All payment processing is handled securely by Apple through the App Store.
  • We do not receive or store your payment information (credit card numbers, billing address, etc.).
  • Apple may collect information as described in their Privacy Policy.

6. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple StoreKit: For subscription management. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

7. Data Security

Since all data processing occurs locally on your device:

  • Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features (Face ID, Touch ID, passcode).
  • We cannot access your data because it never leaves your device.
  • There is no risk of server-side data breaches for your personal information.

8. Children's Privacy

Our App does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The App is designed for general audiences and does not target children.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

10. Your Rights

Since we don't collect your data, traditional data rights (access, deletion, portability) are automatically satisfied - your data stays with you on your device at all times.
